Bio Of Stray Kids

Stray Kids (Korean: 스트레이 키즈; RR: Seuteurei Kijeu; often abbreviated to SKZ) is a South Korean boy band formed by JYP Entertainment. The band consists of eight members

  7. Han (Han Ji-sung)
  8. Hyunjin (Hwang Hyun-jin)
Stray Kids is a korean Kpop band that was been active since 2018 with 8 members currently.
